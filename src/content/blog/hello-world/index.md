# Brainstorming

So playing sim companies and focusing your business on
selling aerospace products is such a pain. The game doesn't have a market to buy aerospace products and see the history of its market prices. So I decided to build a website, where I scrape the in-game chat and use AI to extract the prices and track it. 

There are lots of flaws for using this system, such as players spamming chats with extremely low or high prices. But for now its the only solution I can see to make my buying and selling easier.
